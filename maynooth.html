Maynooth Project

This project was part of Daniel Scott's User Experience Design Essentials course on Udemy.

The objective of the project was to design an online furniture shopping website named Maynooth Furniture, whose competitors were businesses like www.habitat.co.uk, www.made.com, and www.ikea.com.

The persona was a 50-year old executive named Katherine whose children left for university. Now she is looking to remodel her 4 bedroom home and browses style guides online, and she is mostly on Pinterest for her design ideas.

While I was designing the website, I tried to stick with the current habits of customers using websites, and get inspired from the best practices.
